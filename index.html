Instructions

Welcome to the Rope and Cloth Simulator!

  • To create a segment, simply click and drag. 
  • To create a series of attached segments, click and drag from any of the points. 
  • Lock points in place by clicking on them.
  • You can add more segments while paused and the simulation will continue.
  • Arranging segments in a grid pattern will allow you to simulate cloth.
  • Clicking and dragging while simulating will delete segments (This simulates cutting or tearing!).
  • Lastly, you can select one of the pre-defined configuration and add your own segments to it.

Enjoy!
